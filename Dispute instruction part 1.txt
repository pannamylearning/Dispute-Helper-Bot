* When to raise an Escalation:

> 
1st stage escalations raise 5 working days from our flow being sent: Raise level 1 escalation.



> 2nd stage escalations raise 10 working days from our flow being sent. Raise level 2 escalation.



> 3rd stage escalations raise 15 working days from our flow being sent. Raise Contract Manager escalation. 

Escalations required when we have sent the below flows:


Proposalssent >= 5 working days.


Alternatives read proposal >= 5 working days.


Actual Read requests/Disagreements (for want of actual read) >= 10 working days.
